Our Services

Our Services

Search & Clearance

Thorough trademark searches lay the foundation for a strong brand. We examine federal, state and common‑law databases to identify existing marks and potential conflicts.

  • Comprehensive screening of registered and unregistered marks
  • Risk analysis with plain‑language explanations
  • Detailed search reports to guide your filing strategy

Application Filing

Our experienced team prepares and files your trademark application with the USPTO or relevant international office, ensuring every detail is correct.

  • Drafting of identifications and classifications of goods/services
  • Proper specimen and drawing preparation
  • Continual status updates until your application is filed

Monitoring & Enforcement

Protect your valuable brand with our ongoing monitoring and enforcement services. We stay vigilant so you don’t have to.

  • Automated monitoring of new trademark filings
  • Cease‑and‑desist letters and enforcement actions
  • Renewal reminders and portfolio management

Office Action Response

If the USPTO issues an office action, our attorneys craft strategic responses to overcome refusals and objections.

  • Substantive and procedural responses tailored to your application
  • Legal arguments backed by case law and precedent
  • Deadline management to ensure timely filings
